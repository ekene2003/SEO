What is ACP?

The Affordable Connectivity Program (ACP) is a U.S. government program that helps many low income households pay for broadband service and internet connected devices. This sponsorsors $100 to eligibility community on a one time tablet purchase and monthly $35 for data broadband fee for the SIM cards.
